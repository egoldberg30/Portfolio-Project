My name is Eitan (pronounced like the letter A, followed by "tawn"), and I've been creating since the day I was created. Some of my earliest memories are arts and crafts projects. Over the years, I've had many different creative outlets such as painting, drawing, sculpting, film, music, and photography. In high school, I started playing around with Photoshop, and I was instantly hooked. I sold my skills as a service to other students in my school, creating well-edited parody photos for their social media profiles. That experience showed me that my interest in digital design could be a career, not just a hobby. As I researched and experimented with other forms of digitial design, like posters and logos, my interest grew exponentially. I'm now a Graphic Design major and Interactive Digital Media minor at Drexel's Westphal College of Media Arts & Design.
